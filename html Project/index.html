About Summarizer

Two ways of text summarisation: extractive and abstractive. In extractive text summarisation we extract important tokens as sentences from the text and arrange them together, whereas in abstractive we generate a summary that keeps original intent in our own language . But here we have worked upon two extractive based algorithms.

Abstractive Summarization

When a model gives completely different output text as compared to original text or we can say that it produces a new sentence to get the summary

Extractive Summarization

When model extracts valuable tokens/phrases and sentences as output from the original text.Two algorithms we have implemented till now both depend on extractive methodologies. The algorithms we have covered in the project are NLTK and SPACY
